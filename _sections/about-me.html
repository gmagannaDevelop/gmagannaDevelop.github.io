Born and raised in Guadalajara, Jalisco, México. Currently based at León, Guanajuato. I am a Biomedical Engineer, most representatively characterised by his curiosity. I love learning new things, be it languages, math, programming. I picked up my major because I wanted to partake in medical research but my favourite subject was always math. More than merely help "curing" patients, I want my work to help improve the quality of life of chronically ill people like me (I have type 1 diabetes mellitus).

My main interests going into college were math and biology. I had no idea that Computer Science would become such a central topic of interest for me. At the Science and Engineering Division of the University of Guanajuato I had the privilege of working with talented and discilplined scientist who reshaped my vision of a professional career. I was also blessed with encountering friends with an unquenchable intellectual appetite. Together we learnt to program, beyond our programmes' scopes.
